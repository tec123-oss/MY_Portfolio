Clustering Techniques: Grouping Data for Meaningful Insights

Clustering is an unsupervised learning technique used in data mining to group similar data points into clusters. This method helps in discovering hidden patterns in data, making it useful for applications like customer segmentation, anomaly detection, and image processing. Explore different clustering techniques like K-means, DBSCAN, and Hierarchical Clustering to understand how they transform raw data into meaningful insights.
