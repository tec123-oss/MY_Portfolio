The Power of Linear and Logistic Regression in Predictive Analytics

Regression models have been at the core of data science and predictive analytics. In this blog, we will dive into two powerful and widely-used models: Linear Regression and Logistic Regression. Linear Regression is used for predicting continuous values, while Logistic Regression is used for binary classification tasks. We'll explore their mathematical foundations, applications, and compare their strengths and weaknesses to help you determine when to use each model in your analytics projects.
